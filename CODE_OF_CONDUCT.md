# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
FIO community a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of background or identity.

We pledge to act in ways that contribute to an open and healthy community, and to
show empathy and consideration toward others.

## Our standard

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1. The full text is available at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.

In short: be respectful and constructive. Behavior that is demeaning,
discriminatory, or that targets or harasses others is not acceptable.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (issues, pull requests,
discussions) and when an individual is representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer at
**hey@itsdaniel.dk**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and
fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of
reporters.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith may
face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by the project's
leadership.
